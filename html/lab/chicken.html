Spicy Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Step 2: Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a few minutes.
  3. Step 3: Add tomatoes, curry powder, and chili powder. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Step 4: Add chicken and cook until done. Serve hot.

Serving Suggestions

Enjoy with steamed rice or warm naan bread!
